LunaPlate Privacy Policy

Effective July 14, 2026

LunaPlate is a cycle-care planning tool, not a medical device. Its estimates and suggestions do not replace professional medical advice.

What stays on your device

Period dates, daily check-ins, flow, mood, pain score, hydration, notes, notification choices, and cycle settings are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData. LunaPlate does not require an account. You can export a JSON backup or delete all local data from the Profile screen.

Data sent for recommendations

When the recommendation service is reachable, the app sends only the current estimated cycle phase and the symptom identifiers you selected to the LunaPlate server. These values are used to return meal and movement suggestions. The current service does not write these requests to an application database or build a user profile. If the service is unavailable, the app uses its bundled offline catalog.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell personal or health data.
  • We do not use advertising SDKs or cross-app tracking.
  • We do not request precise location, contacts, photos, or HealthKit access.
  • We do not use your cycle data to make automated medical decisions.

Public content providers

The server may retrieve public recipe content from TheMealDB. Only a meal search is sent to that provider; your local cycle history and notes are not sent. Evidence links in care cards open authoritative public health websites in your browser.

Security and retention

Production requests use HTTPS. Because no account exists, we cannot retrieve or delete data stored only on your device; use the in-app delete control or remove the app. Exported backups are controlled by the location and service you choose when saving them.

Children and changes

LunaPlate is not directed to children under 13. We may update this policy when app behavior changes and will revise the effective date above.

Contact

For privacy questions or deletion concerns about the server, open an issue in the LunaPlate project repository.

隐私政策摘要

经期、症状、疼痛、情绪、饮水和笔记保存在设备本地。在线推荐只发送当前推算阶段与所选症状标识，不发送完整周期历史或笔记。应用无广告、无跨应用追踪，也不会出售健康数据。你可以在“个人资料”中导出备份或删除全部本地数据。
